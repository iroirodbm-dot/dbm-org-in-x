Our Mission

Deaf Biblical Ministry (DBM), a registered society under the Society Registration Act, 1860, is a National Award-winning NGO recognized for its pioneering work and profound impact on the welfare of the Deaf in Nagaland and beyond. Established in 1987, DBM is the first and remains the only organization of its kind in Nagaland, dedicated to running a School for the Deaf in Dimapur.

With a vision to provide quality education, equip the Deaf with essential skills for independent living, and serve as a beacon of empowerment, DBM continues to transform lives and uplift the Deaf community. DBM has received three President of Indiaâ€™s National Awards and is the pioneer school for the Deaf in India and the only Deaf Church in North East India.

Currently, DBM provides free education and accommodation to 73 Deaf children and over 200 Deaf Church members.
